Our Story

Suraksha Alert was founded with a vision to revolutionize disaster management in India using cutting-edge technology and community engagement. We believe that timely information and proper preparation can save countless lives during emergency situations.

Our Mission

At Suraksha Alert, our mission is to provide timely, accurate, and actionable information during emergencies, empowering individuals and communities to respond effectively to disasters.

We work tirelessly to develop innovative solutions that connect people with critical resources, emergency services, and each other during times of crisis. By leveraging technology, data analytics, and community networks, we aim to minimize the impact of disasters and save lives.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no life is lost due to lack of information or resources during disasters. We believe in building communities that are prepared, connected, and resilient in the face of emergencies.

Our goal is to create a comprehensive disaster management ecosystem that integrates early warning systems, real-time coordination, and post-disaster recovery support. By 2030, we aim to protect 100 million people across India with our advanced alert systems.

Our Values

Reliability: We provide accurate information when it matters most. Our systems undergo rigorous testing to ensure they work flawlessly during emergencies.

Accessibility: We believe life-saving tools should be available to everyone, regardless of location, language, or technical proficiency.

Innovation: We constantly improve our technology to better serve communities, incorporating the latest advancements in AI, IoT, and data analytics.

Compassion: We put people first in everything we do, designing our services with empathy for those in vulnerable situations.

Our Journey

Week 1

In this week, we finalized our teammates and discussed the 17 SDGs and decided to focus on our major goal and think in the same direction to finalise our idea, we discussed various ideas and the positives and negatives of working on them.

Week 2

In this week, we decided to cater to the problem of road accidents and road safety majorly concerning natural disasters especially in hilly areas as it a major problem in those terrains and faced by one of our teammates during his travel journey.

Week 3

We finalized our Project Title and discussed about what major issues will be solved by our project called Suraksha Alert. We drafted a problem statement and listed the concerns which are faced by the real world in real time and how important is to be solved.

Week 4

This week was our week of research and feasibility study. We studied about the disaster management needs and studied the popularly used apps like google maps and waze. We tried to find gaps in the existing solutions and visited the website of road and transport ministry to know the real statistics of the drivers who actually face these problems.We successfully submitted our milestone 1 in this period.

Week 5

This week was the project planning and execution mapping week. We finalized the features we want to provide to our users. We completed our research work by visiting the official website NDRF and studied the competition apps like 112 and studies about the reasons behind the failure of these apps. We reached out to various seniors and talked to industry experts about the project and had a chance to present our idea in IDRIS(International Skill Development and Research Innovation Summit ) and received various forms of feedbacks which were essential to provide a direction to our planning.
