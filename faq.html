Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Suraksha Alert

General Questions

What is Suraksha Alert?

Suraksha Alert is a comprehensive disaster management and emergency response platform designed to help individuals, communities, and organizations prepare for, respond to, and recover from natural and man-made disasters. Our services include early warning systems, emergency alerts, location tracking, evacuation planning, and resource coordination.

How does Suraksha Alert work?

Suraksha Alert uses advanced technology to monitor potential disaster situations and provide timely alerts to users. Our platform collects data from various sources, including weather services, seismic monitoring stations, and government agencies, to predict and track disasters. When a potential threat is detected, we send alerts to users in the affected areas and provide guidance on safety measures and evacuation routes.

Is Suraksha Alert available in my area?

Suraksha Alert is currently available throughout India, with plans to expand to other countries in the near future. Our coverage is most comprehensive in urban areas, but we are continuously working to improve our services in rural regions as well. Please check our coverage map on the main page for specific details about your location.

Account & Registration

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple. Download the Suraksha Alert app from the App Store or Google Play Store, or visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. You'll need to provide some basic information, including your name, email address, phone number, and location. Once you've completed the registration form, you'll receive a verification code via SMS or email to activate your account.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely on our servers. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or in emergency situations where sharing your location with emergency services may be necessary. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Can I use Suraksha Alert without creating an account?

While some basic features, such as viewing general disaster alerts and safety tips, are available without an account, most of our services require registration. This is because we need your location and contact information to provide personalized alerts and emergency assistance. Creating an account also allows you to set up emergency contacts and customize your alert preferences.

Alerts & Notifications

How will I receive alerts?

Alerts are delivered through multiple channels to ensure you receive them even in challenging conditions. These include push notifications through our app, SMS text messages, email alerts, and automated phone calls for critical emergencies. You can customize your preferred notification methods in your account settings.

Can I customize which types of alerts I receive?

Yes, you can customize your alert preferences in the app or website settings. You can choose which types of disasters you want to be notified about (e.g., floods, earthquakes, fires) and set the severity threshold for alerts. However, we recommend keeping all critical alerts enabled for your safety.

What should I do when I receive an alert?

When you receive an alert, stay calm and follow the instructions provided. The alert will include information about the nature of the emergency, its severity, and recommended actions. Depending on the situation, you may be advised to evacuate, seek shelter, or take other protective measures. The app will also provide access to evacuation routes, emergency contact information, and safety guidelines specific to the type of disaster.

Technical Support

What if the app isn't working during an emergency?

We've designed Suraksha Alert to function even in challenging conditions, including limited internet connectivity. However, if you experience issues with the app during an emergency, you'll still receive alerts via SMS and phone calls if you've provided your contact information. We also recommend downloading offline maps and emergency guides in advance, which are accessible without an internet connection.

How can I report a bug or technical issue?

If you encounter any technical issues or bugs, please report them through the "Help & Support" section in the app or website. You can also email our technical support team at support@surakshaalert.com. Please include as much detail as possible, including your device model, operating system version, and a description of the issue, to help us resolve it quickly.

Does Suraksha Alert work without internet access?

Some features of Suraksha Alert require internet access to function properly, such as real-time alerts and location tracking. However, we've designed the app to work with limited connectivity. Critical alerts can be delivered via SMS, and we provide offline access to emergency guides, first aid instructions, and previously downloaded maps. We recommend downloading these resources in advance when you have a stable internet connection.
